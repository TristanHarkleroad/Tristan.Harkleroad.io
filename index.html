About Me

My name is Tristan Harkleroad and I am currently a student in the Georgia Tech Coding
Bootcamp. I was born and raised in Savannah, Georgia and currently live in Midway, Georgia.
I graduated from Benedictine Military School in 2014 and went on to graduate from the Citadel
in 2018. I discovered my passion for computer coding and programming when I was a second
semester senior in college. Once I figured it out, I started researching and learning about coding.
My brother told me about this coding bootcamp for Georgia Tech and I looked into it.
And here I am!

My hobbies consist of competitive gaming, Netflix, writing code, and reading. I love to watch
Game of Thrones, Stranger Things, The Office, and Marvel movies. I enjoy learning new things
and keeping up with new technology being developed and how it improved from past versions.
I am pretty open to anyone and I am very easy to get along with too. I try to be a friend to
everyone I meet because everyone needs a good friend. I hope you enjoy my first website!
